OHH YES,
I AM SINGLE.

and so is my girlfriend.

A story that hurts a little… and jokes about it anyway.

This is not a tragic love story.


It just behaves like one… sometimes.


You meet someone. You like them. You build small routines.


And then one day… it slips.


Not loudly. Not dramatically. Just silence.


The book doesn’t cry about it. It laughs.


But the kind of laugh that knows something broke.

Read this if—


  • • you check your phone for someone who’s not coming back
  • • you replay conversations in your head
  • • nights feel heavier than they should


This book doesn’t fix you.


It makes sense of you.

YOU DON’T MISS THEM.

YOU MISS WHO YOU WERE
WITH THEM.

They don’t write perfect love.
They write what actually happens.

Not every story ends.


Some just… stop.


And maybe that’s where yours begins.

You won’t notice it at first.


The way you stop checking your phone. The way their name feels lighter.


Not gone. Just quieter.


And one day… it won’t hurt the same.

if you’re still thinking about it…
